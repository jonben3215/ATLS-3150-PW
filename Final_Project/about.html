How To Achieve Universal Design

Creating things that everyone can use means making them accessible to all kinds of people. To do this, include everyone's needs right from the start. Learn about different people's needs by talking to them and doing research. Make designs that are flexible, simple, and clear so that many people can use them easily. Use tools that help, easy-to-understand layouts, and designs that are comfortable for everyone. Think about diversity and inclusion from the beginning, not just as an extra. Keep testing with different people and getting feedback to make things better for everyone.

Tools Used In Universal Design

There are three pivotal tools that were especially helpful in our class: the W3 Validator, WAVE Extension, and HTML5 Outliner. These tools played a crucial role in ensuring accessibility and inclusivity in our digital designs.
